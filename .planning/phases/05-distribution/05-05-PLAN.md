| PR fork branches → workflow | `pull_request: branches: [main]` runs with the standard restricted token (PR-fork limitation). `pull_request_target` NOT used. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-05-15 | Tampering | compromised third-party action | medium | mitigate | Pin to major version (@v4/@v5/@v6/@v7). Dependabot (Plan 06) opens weekly PRs for upgrades. SHA-pinning deferred to v0.2.x. |
| T-05-16 | EoP | workflow runs with excessive permissions | medium | mitigate | Top-level `permissions: contents: read` (default least). `id-token: write` only on the test job (required for Codecov OIDC). NO `attestations: write` or `contents: write` in this workflow. |
| T-05-17 | Information Disclosure | Codecov token leaked in logs | low | accept | OIDC (`use_oidc: true`) eliminates the long-lived token entirely. GITHUB_TOKEN is auto-redacted by the runner. |
